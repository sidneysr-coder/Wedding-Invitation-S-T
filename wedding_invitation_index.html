Our Story

We met in 2023 at the Whiskey River West bar. Tristan was looking to dance, and I was out with a group of friends. The moment I saw Tristan, I knew I had to dance with him — so I made sure he noticed me and asked me to dance. We danced one song, then two more. Later in the night, he came back and asked me to dance again, and we ended up dancing until the place was about to close.

Tristan asked for my number, and I gave it to him thinking I would never see him again — I assumed he was just visiting for business. Little did I know that he actually lived in Katy, and that he felt the same connection I did. The next day (or so), he texted me and invited me on our first date... and from that moment on, we’ve been inseparable.
